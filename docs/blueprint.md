# **App Name**: Lumina Online

## Core Features:

- Interactive Homepage: An immersive homepage featuring parallax scrolling to showcase featured products and promotions.
- Product Dashboard: A central hub for managing and displaying products, with clear categorization and search functionality.
- Product Detail Page: Detailed product pages with high-quality visuals, descriptions, customer reviews, and clear call-to-action buttons. Option to suggest related or similar products, powered by AI.
- Products Page: A comprehensive listing of all products, filterable by category, price, and other relevant attributes. It could have infinite scrolling implemented for improved experience.
- Quick View: A modal window providing essential product information and an add-to-cart option without navigating to the full product page.
- Light/Dark Mode Toggle: A user-controlled toggle to switch between light and dark color schemes for optimal viewing experience in various lighting conditions.
- Personalized Recommendations Tool: Generative AI powered product recommendations based on browsing history and purchase behavior.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Midnight Blue (#191970) to convey elegance and sophistication.
- Background color: Dark Gray (#282828) for dark mode, Light Gray (#EEEEEE) for light mode, both lightly desaturated from the primary hue.
- Accent color: Electric Indigo (#6F00FF) to highlight interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font.
- Sleek, minimalist icons with a thin stroke style for a modern, uncluttered look.
- Use of white space and clean lines to create a sense of sophistication.
- Subtle animations and transitions, especially for the parallax scrolling effect and loading states.
